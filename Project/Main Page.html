About Just Cuts

Just Cuts is your local barbershop. We have been around for 20 years and have more than a collective 60 years of service amongst our barbers that we proudly use to serve the people of Dallas.

We are staffed by 6 talented barbers in Dallas. We use traditional means to create a barbershop environment that will ease you in as you recieve the best cut experience. And if you want a shave or waxing to help complete the experience, we can off that.

We specialize in men's hair cuts, ball fades, shaving, various waxes, and all sorts of old fashion methods. Yet we do not lose sight of modern methods while keeping the old aesthetic. Our barbershops are experienced and waiting!
